About Us

The USIU Lost & Found System is a trusted platform dedicated to reuniting students, faculty, and staff with their lost belongings. Our mission is to enhance campus security and convenience by providing an efficient and transparent process for reporting and retrieving lost items.

Through technology-driven solutions and a community-first approach, we ensure that every item is accounted for and returned in a timely manner. Our system is built to minimize losses and provide peace of mind to our university community.
